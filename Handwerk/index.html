Six steps to succsessful cooperation
Step 01
Sign up
Register in just two minutes with your name and your company data
  • After entering your personal data and your general company data, we will send you an email
  • Please confirm the link in the email, so that we know that it is you
  • Now you decide only if you want to get started with our basic membership, or if you want to use all benefits of the Plus membership directly and you can start in the member area
Click here for our terms and conditions and our privacy policy
Good to know!
The membership fees at Handwerk Connected are tax deductible
Step 02
Create your company profile
Complete your company profile in the member area
  • In order to be able to fully use Handwerk Connected, we still need some information about the competences in your company.
  • With the aim of successful K, it is important to start all the necessary infor- opo operations, to deposit information such as expertise, manufacturer experience, etc. at the employee level.
  • This includes, among other things, the definition of the area in which you are looking for or would like to offer.
Good to know!
The membership fees at Handwerk Connected are tax deductible
Step 03
Search / Offer
Specify which product or professional you are looking for or want to share on the network
  • Now you can narrow down very narrowly which person with which qualification or which support from which foreign trade you want to search or offer temporarily
  • The filters refer to eg place of use, date of application, discipline and qualification. The complete overview of the filter options you will find after your registration in the members area
Good to know!
The membership fees at Handwerk Connected are tax deductible
Step 04
Select cooperation company
Choose the offer that suits you or accept a request
  • During the search you will be shown a list of companies that have the right offer for you.
  • You decide which company you want to contact.
  • If you have made an offer, the colleagues ask you directly and you decide with whom you would like to cooperate.
Step 05
Make contact
Get in contact with your future cooperation partner
  • Now contact us to discuss the details of your cooperation.
  • If it fits in personally and in terms of content, you can start.
  • The contact takes place, of course, only from owner to owner.
Good to know!
The membership fees at Handwerk Connected are tax deductible
Step 06
Specialists forum use
Use the knowledge of others in the Specialist Forum
  • Exchange with others about difficult issues and current topics from the craft
  • Stay up to date on news
