What makes us different?

The bundling of different competencies and experiences. Our team draws on extensive knowledge and diverse experiences from different industries. We are proud that we can now apply this knowledge for craft Connected and use.

The advantages at a glance:

The craft is in transition. Imagine over 500,000 craft businesses with a multiple of employees would be networked and cooperate. No less is our goal!

Handwerk Connected will focus on the sectors SHK and Elektro in the first step. Other craft industries will follow in the middle of the year.

The team behind handwerk connected
Natascha Swientek, Andreas Urban, Manuel Fitzner und Nicole Dunker

André Swientek

Founder & Consultant

André has over 16 years of experience in advising personnel services for international corporations. He previously worked as a product manager in the industry for just as long and started his working life with an apprenticeship as an electrician.

The situation of skilled labor shortage and his accumulated expertise have driven him to bring together the advantages of all areas.

"That which brings success in other industries with flexibility of the team, also works in the craft. When the craft gets connected, it gets stronger in itself. "

He made the start for Handwerk Connected possible and set important impulses.

André has no active role in Handwerk Connected. If required, however, he will continue to provide advice to the team with his expertise in the area of personnel services.

Nicole Dunker

Founder and Managing Director

Nicole has more than 12 years of experience with a well-known heating manufacturer in the areas of marketing, sales, e-commerce and digitization. During this time, she brought some new momentum to the market and successfully implemented it. She always had a lot of fun with a direct exchange with the craft and the market:

"Over the last few years, I have seen first-hand how many experts from SHK Handwerk experienced the problem of skills shortages in the craft trades. Accordingly, the pain that the subject brings with it increases. Now is the time to solve these challenges through new ways - that's what we at Handwerk Connected will do together with the craft. "

Nicole is part of the founding team of Handwerk Connected and also leads our HC team as Managing Director.

Andreas is not only a founding member of Handwerk Connected, but also responsible for all technical topics of our cooperation platform as Head of IT & Development.

Our drive and mission statement

As a service provider for the skilled trades, we want to solve the industry's biggest problem, the shortage of skilled workers, via our cooperation platform.

Our motivation is our mission statement, which we believe in with all consequences:

  • We believe in the power of cooperation
  • We believe in the strength and sustainability of the craft
  • We believe that the specialized and flexible deployment of professionals is part of the solution to the growing skills shortage
  • We are convinced that the combination of new ways and proven traditions promises success
