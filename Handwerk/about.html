SOLUTION AGAINST FAX EMERGENCY

The craft is in transition.

Shortage of skilled labor, digitization, falling apprenticeship numbers in craft trades, changed business models and processes are all issues that you as a farmer can not avoid today and in the future in order to continue to be successful.

Of course you are not alone with that!

We offer as a service provider for the craft solutions to the topic of shortage of skilled workers and would like to develop these together with you.

Together in the future of the craft

As a member of Handwerk Connected, you can always access a network of colleagues and benefit from their experience and, above all, share and search employee resources in the network. Your advantages are obvious.

The larger the number of companies in the network, the stronger the craft will become by itself. Find and offer specialists and specialists and profit from the sum of the experiences of your colleagues. Connect with others and increase the profitability of your company.
